Our Story

From a standing start, we have forged forward and become one of Harleton Down High Road's most recognised independent Estate agents. Trading now for over 10 years and withstanding the downturn of the property market over the last few years we have learned lessons that have made us determined and resilient. We offer a comprehensive service, whether you want to let your property, attain the best price for your house or get a mortgage for your dream home. We believe being backed by high level marketing and advertising as well as striving to attain first class customer service will hopefully prove well for the next ten years and allow us to be a good all round property service provider.

We pride ourselves on getting to know each property, which enables us to match your home with the buyer best suited to your situation. Our ultimate priority is to achieve the best price for you, with a swift and smooth exchange and hassle free completion.
